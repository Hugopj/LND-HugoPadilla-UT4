THAMES TINTS

THAMES TINTS sunglasses are a stylish and practical accessory that is perfect for anyone looking to add a touch of sophistication to their outfit while also protecting their eyes from the sun's harmful rays.


Handcrafted in Yorkshire, these sunglasses are made with the utmost care and attention to detail to ensure that they meet the highest standards of quality.

The frame of the glasses is a beautiful beige color, which is versatile enough to complement any outfit, whether it's casual or formal. However, what really sets these sunglasses apart are the small details.


The frame of the glasses features two little orange stones, carefully embedded on each side. These stones add a pop of color and sparkle to the glasses, which catch the light and create a unique and striking look.
